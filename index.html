About the Artist

Where light
meets story

Hi, I'm Janaki — a nature and landscape photographer with a deep passion for the transient moments that define our natural world — the last embers of a sunset, the silent explosion of a flower in bloom, the alien geometry of an insect's eye.

Every photograph is a conversation between patience and presence. I chase the extraordinary hidden within the ordinary, from the vast canvas of a sky to the intricate universe found on a single leaf.
